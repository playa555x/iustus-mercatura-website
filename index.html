At Iustus Mercatura, sustainability is not just a goal - it's the foundation of everything we do. We are committed to creating lasting positive impact across our entire supply chain.

A Message from our

Chief Executive Officer

Dear Partners, Stakeholders, and Friends,

We have not only positioned the Holding Company as a strategic management holding, but also moved away from purely trading in food commodities towards establishing the complete supply chain - from the field to the end customer.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create transparent, sustainable, and efficient trade networks that benefit all participants in the supply chain. Today, we stand proud of what we have built together.

The Iustus Mercatura Group now operates across multiple continents, with offices in key strategic locations including the British Virgin Islands, USA, UAE, UK, Brazil, and East Africa. Our diverse portfolio spans agricultural commodities, energy trading, and maritime logistics.

Looking ahead, we remain committed to our founding principles of integrity, excellence, and partnership. We continue to invest in sustainable practices and digital innovation to better serve our clients and communities.

With warm regards,

Dr. Gerhard Kral
